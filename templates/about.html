About

This system is part of our degree project in the course DA150X at KTH, Royal Institute of Technology.

Purpose

Writing questions for fellow students has proven beneficial for student learning. The purpose of this study is to investigate how a question-authoring system could be constructed for use in university courses.

Timeframe

The study will end on May 2nd 2021. After that we will send out a survey where you will be asked about your experience with the system.

Questions or issues

If you have any questions about the study or the system, or if you are experiencing any issues, please contact us at: mnordste@kth.se or oskaredv@kth.se
