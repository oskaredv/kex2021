Points and Badges
  • Points: As a reward for submitting questions you will earn points
  • Streak: If you submit questions several days in a row you will get a streak
  • Streak point boost: While having an active streak you will get more points for submitting a question
  • Badges: You can also earn badges for submitting certain amounts of questions or for maintaining a streak

To view your points and badges, head over to your profile page!
